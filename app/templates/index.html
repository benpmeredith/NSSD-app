The Lantern

The National School Security Database (NSSD)

A NoSchoolViolence.org Project

Use the Lantern to discover how certain adolescent behaviors are related to several kinds of school violence. This information can help parents, teachers, students, school staff and counsellors, mental health professionals, educational policy analysts, and researchers better understand the connection between behavioral patterns and youth violence. These insights can help create individual student or school wide intervention programs.
